About Us


Machado General Construction was created by Douglas Machado, a Brazilian, who came to the USA 20 years ago, where he specialized and later founded the construction company, where in honor of his family he named it "Machado General Construction".


The construction company was created with the aim of helping people to build their dream property, following from the plan, to the completion of the property.


We have state-of-the-art equipment to perform the service as quickly as possible and deliver the service rich in detail.


Remains of materials are disposed of in a suitable place, in order to protect the environment.


Our employees have been trained to deliver quality service to everyone.


Machado General Construction is a company that has been growing more and more, and every day, realizing a dream. Let us do yours too!
